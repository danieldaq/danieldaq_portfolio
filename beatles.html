THE BEATLES

Hand-Crafted Form & Color Book

I love projects that involve being hands-on, so creating this book about The Beatles was a process I thoroughly enjoyed.

One of the biggest challenges of creating this 8" x 8" accordion-fold book was incorporating elements of physical interaction between the viewer and the book itself.

I decided on having the book flow in chronological order of the Beatles' musical career, having each page highlight a point in their career or giving attention to one of the Beatles.

In the process of making the book, some of the pages became simple paper cut-outs while other pages incorporated lift-up pages and pop-ups for interaction. However, showing the interplay of form and color was the biggest element in keeping the viewer's overall engagement.

STEAMPUNK AVIATOR

Illustration

The artistic style and context of Steampunk has always fascinated me, reminding me of a hybrid between a grungy Victorian Era and art nouveau that fictional characters can thrive in. This project took on an illustrative approach towards creating my own steampunk character.

Starting off with the character's accessories, I ended up with sketches of a giant wrench, tool belt, goggles and aviation hat. Eventually, everything came together to make this Steampunk Aviator.

Rather than using gradients, I created solid shapes to convey shading and depth within the final look of the character.

Wanting to explore different looks, I also created monochromatic, complementary, and analogous color versions of Mr. Aviator.

This was a great project that allowed me to bring my illustration abilities into vector form, even when it comes to the smaller details.
